    Till now we have used the transformation that are cartesian. But for the axi-symmetric, the trnasformation has to be cylindrical, and this is given at the top of the slide.

    After solving the governign equations, we could obtain the heat transfer rate. We are interested to know the heat transfer rate because, in stagnation region temperature will be higher, specially in the case of hypersnoic flow. (exmaple the re entry vehicle).

    The solutionn for different axi symmetrical body is given below. For the case of Cylinder, and Sphere. We see that both of them are same except for the coefficents. The first one, which is cylinder is 0.57, and the next one which is sphere is 0.763. So this says thatHeat transfer rate is higher in the case of sphere when compared to a cylinder for the same given free stream condition.

    And this is due to famous Three-dimensional relieving effect. Because of this Thickness of boundary layer will be higher for sphere when compared to the thckness for the case of cylinder.
    And if the thickness is smaller, temperature gradient wil be larger. And thats the reaosn for higher Heat transfer rate for sphere compared to cylinder.

    Another thing to note here is that velocity gradient appears in both the expression of cylinder and sphere.
